Welcome

You've found the official page for Team zero Gaming. We're a small eSports team made up of three primary members and a mix of our friends who pop in and out as we play. We started during the pandemic in 2020 when my son thought it would be cool to be on an eSports team but didn't think he would be good enough for it. My response was "Well, lets make our own then," and we did.

Rules

So I'm not a big fan of having lots of rules but over time there's a few that we have added just to make sure everyone can have a good time. So whenever you are playing with us please follow this list of rules:

  • Everyone is allowed to play with us

    Team zero Gaming was born out of others excluding younger players from joining an eSports team. We didn't like being left out of the fun so we won't do that to anyone else unless you give us a reason to.

  • Respect everyone

    We understand that some people are more sensitive than others and eventually even the most careful person says something that will offend someone. Just apologize and move on. Remember if it's not funny for all of us, its not funny. Yes, you'll probably hear zero rage and let our some... colorful language, but cussing out someone who's playing with us isn't ok.

  • Your loot is your loot

    So every game is a little different on this but we include it for when it applies. In a game like Fortnite, it's all about who grabs what first. Expecting one person to be the sole owner of a loot crate is unrealistic because individuals who aren't part of Team zero Gaming aren't going to observe that either. In a game like Minecraft though, it can be really frustrating if you spend forever searching for loot, while someone else is just messing around or worse not even online, and you hit the jackpot with loot only for someone who made zero contribution to come and take it without talking to you first. Honestly this should all be assumed with "Respect everyone" but we've has some issues with this.

So really to sum it all up, basically "Respect everyone" is our only rule. The other two are specific applications of that rule that we have encountered problems with in the past. We hope to not make this list longer but reserve the right to block anyone at any time for any reason though we rarely ever do. Have fun playing

Games We Play

Fortnite

Fortnite is a battle royal game. Usually you can find us playing in Duos, Squads, or Team Rumble game modes and occasionally on the weekends well join kfen in his custom tournaments. This is our most played game on stream because it's free to play so z2's friends can all play with us and it's cartoon like style makes it a bit more friendly for the younger players who play with us.

Minecraft

Minecraft is one of z2's favorite games. Minecraft has a lot of gathering resources and building things. While not a free game this one is pretty common with younger players so again we can play it with z2's friends

Destiny 2

Destiny 2 is a first person shooter MMO. Destiny isn't as kiddy as some of the other games we play but the goal of Team zero Gaming is allowing anyone to play with us not being a kids team. This is zero's game of choice though you won't often find him in the PVP matches. He prefers the PVE events and story levels.
