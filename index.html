Welcome Meet the Team

Team zero Gaming

Welcome

You've found the official page for Team zero Gaming. We're a small eSports team made up of three primary members and a mix of our friends who pop in and out as we play. We started during the pandemic in 2020 when my son thought it would be cool to be on an eSports team but didn't think he would be good enough for it. My response was "Well, lets make our own then," and we did.

Meet the Team!

#01

zeroone4004

The epic leader of Team zero Gaming and the one who started it all. Usually pretty chill but expect some raging if z2 talks him into doing a death run in Fortnite.

Favorite Games: Astroneer, Factorio, Destiny 2, Fortnite \(if z2 is playing\), pretty much every Zelda game, and many more

Zeroone4004, also known as "zero," was the first member of Team zero Gaming. The other two are zero's kids who thought zero's gamertag was awesome and decided to be zerotwo4004 and zerothree4004. Zero plays the widest variety of games including Fortnite, Factorio, Astroneer, Destiny 2, and more. He is the account manager for the Team zero Gaming twitch stream, YouTube channel, and Facebook page.

Zerotwo4004 was the second member to join Team zero Gaming. Usually you can find us playing with two or three other people who are usually friends of z2. Z2 usually plays Fortnite but also plays Minecraft frequently. He sometimes talks zero into playing Minecraft with him too.

#02

zerotwo4004

Son of the original zero. Ofter recruits his friends to play with Team zero. Likes to laugh and have a good time. One of his favorite things is laughing at zero's frustration while doing death runs.

Favorite Games: Fortnite, Minecraft

#03

zerothree4004

The youngest member of Team zero. Decided to follow her brother z2 in the zero naming scheme when choosing her gamer tag. Usually seen sitting with zero rather than playing herself.

Favorite game: Mario Kart

Zerothree4004 is the youngest of the zeros and had been playing with them the least. Zerothree4004 is a brain cancer survivor and started playing with Team zero Gaming during one of her chemo treatments. She is in full remission now. Her game of choice is Mario Kart though she got her start playing with the team by playing Fortnite with zero while he was with her in the hospital.
